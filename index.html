Abulele Yekani (SPOTZ)

Abulele Yekani, performing under the stage name SPOTZ, is a highly talented young artist from Cape Town, South Africa. He has been gaining recognition for his exceptional musical abilities and unique sound. Known for his deep and powerful style, SPOTZ’s productions demonstrate a maturity beyond his years, showcasing his innovative approach to music.

His work reflects a distinct fusion of intricate sound design and emotive energy, establishing him as a noteworthy figure in the South African music scene. Given Cape Town’s rich cultural and artistic heritage, SPOTZ’s rising prominence is indicative of the city’s continuing contribution to global music trends.
